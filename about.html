BLKVI

Private protection, professionally managed.

BLKVI serves principals, executives, families, and public-facing clients who expect protection to be competent, discreet, and professionally managed from the first call forward. Founded by Kevin Jeffery, the firm is supported by a team of professionals aligned to the same standard of readiness, conduct, and client care.

Foundation

Founded with a clear operating standard

Kevin Jeffery founded BLKVI to offer private protection that is organized, discreet, and professionally executed. The standard is not built around theater. It is built around preparation, judgment, and the ability to move clients through complex environments without unnecessary exposure or disruption.

Team standard

A team aligned to that standard

BLKVI is supported by professionals who are selected and trusted to work within that framework. Clients are not hiring a personality. They are retaining a firm whose team is expected to operate with consistency, discretion, respect, and composure in private, corporate, and high-visibility settings.
